About the Project

The Kangroo App is a cutting-edge shopping application developed using Swift and Xcode. Designed to provide a seamless and fast shopping experience, the app integrates innovative features to enhance user convenience and satisfaction.

Key Features:

  • User Authentication: Includes secure login and user creation with validation to ensure data accuracy and safety.
  • Change Password Feature: Allows users to securely update their password, enhancing account security.
  • User Data Storage: Stores user information in a secure and encrypted format to ensure privacy and protection.
  • Top-Up Feature: Allows users to add funds quickly by simply tapping on the top-up option.
  • Shake for Discount: Users can shake their device during checkout to unlock special discounts, adding an element of fun and engagement.
  • Quick Purchase: A "Buy Now" feature enables users to purchase a single item quickly without adding it to the cart.
  • Cart Functionality:
    • Add items to the cart.
    • Purchase all items in the cart with a single action.
    • Sort cart items based on various filters for better organization.
  • Delivery or Pickup Options: Users can choose between delivery or pickup based on their preference.
  • Weather-Based Recommendations: The app suggests delivery or pickup based on the current weather conditions, ensuring user convenience.

The Kangroo App stands out for its innovative features and user-centric design, making it an ideal choice for fast and efficient shopping experiences.
