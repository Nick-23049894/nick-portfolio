Dine or Dash: Enhancing Your Dining Experience

Figma Video

Overview:

Dine or Dash is a high-fidelity prototype created in Figma to visualize an innovative mobile application that simplifies the entire dining process. Whether you're ordering, picking up, or opting for delivery, the app serves as a unified platform to enhance convenience and efficiency, connecting users with a wide variety of restaurants.

Key Features:

  • Unified Platform: Consolidates ordering, pickup, and delivery across multiple restaurants into one app.
  • Smart Search and Recommendations: Intelligently suggests meals and restaurants tailored to user preferences.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Provides up-to-date tracking of orders for complete transparency.
  • Customizable Orders: Offers intuitive customization options for menu items to suit individual tastes.
  • Exclusive Promotions: Includes redeemable promo codes and coupons for additional savings.

Design Highlights:

  • Figma Auto-Layout:
    • Home Page: Displays restaurant cards in an engaging vertical layout.
    • Menu: Features horizontally scrolling categories and vertically scrolling menu items.
    • Item Customization: Easy vertical scrolling for personalization options.
    • Cart and Receipt Pages: Seamlessly showcase cart items, order information, and receipts in vertical scrolls.
    • Promos: Coupons are presented in a clean, vertical layout for easy browsing.

Interactive Features in Prototype:

  • Triggers and Animations:
    • Smooth transitions with push, slide-in, and smart-animate effects.
    • On-tap triggers for intuitive navigation and interaction.
  • Conditional Logic Simulation:
    • Dynamic cart management (e.g., removing items when their quantity is set to zero).
    • Promo redemption tracking to ensure a smooth and logical user flow.

Prototype Variables for Interactivity:

  • BeefQty & ChickenQty: Simulate tracking and adjusting item quantities.
  • Redeemed: Manages promo redemption states to demonstrate conditional logic.
  • ItemPrice: Dynamically calculates costs in the cart for realism.

Goal:

This prototype was designed in Figma to demonstrate how a streamlined app can create a hassle-free dining experience for busy users. It focuses on convenience, personalization, and efficiency while showcasing an intuitive user interface and interactive design.
