Design Rationale

I’ve created a unique set of stickers inspired by poker cards, reflecting my personality and reactions to various situations. Each sticker is designed with a specific suit and emotion in mind, showcasing different aspects of my personality:

  • Clubs: "Silent Judgement" - for when I’m left speechless.
  • Spades: "Haha, no answer" - my response to tough questions.
  • Diamonds: "I’m so done" - when I’ve reached my limit and need a break.
  • Hearts: "Confused" - my expression when asked to do something unfamiliar.
  • King: "Evil Smile" - representing my mischievous side.

The decision to use poker card icons stems from my love for card games, which I wanted to incorporate into my design. These stickers not only express my emotions but also add a touch of personality to my interactions, making them both relatable and fun!
