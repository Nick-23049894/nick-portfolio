Overview of the Game

Genre: VR Escape Room

Inspirational References:

  • Skyrim VR
  • Escape Simulator

Basic Narrative:

Imprisoned in a medieval castle, the player's only hope of escape lies in exploration, puzzle-solving, and crafting essential items. By gathering resources and crafting keys, they can unlock doors, overcome obstacles, and solve puzzles. Each challenge conquered brings the player closer to freedom. However, the castle's secrets are well-guarded, requiring wits and resourcefulness to escape.

Unique Selling Points

"Dungeon Escape" stands out in the VR escape room genre with its immersive storytelling, intricate puzzles, and richly detailed medieval environment. The game integrates narrative and gameplay, ensuring players uncover the story of their imprisonment as they progress. Key features include:

  • Teleportation Locomotion: Maximizes player comfort by reducing motion sickness, ensuring an intuitive exploration experience.
  • Crafting System: Adds depth to gameplay by allowing players to gather resources and craft items, blending escape room mechanics with survival elements.
  • Low Poly Art Style: The medieval dungeon's unique low-poly design creates an atmospheric and visually distinct environment.

Game Loop

Detailed Walkthrough:

  1. Spawn in the Room: Player starts in the first room of the medieval castle.
  2. Explore the Room: Look around, examine objects, and find clues.
  3. Collect Items: Gather resources for puzzle-solving.
  4. Solve Puzzles: Unlock new areas by solving challenges.
  5. Progress to Next Room: Repeat the process in each room.
  6. Final Escape: Solve the last puzzle to escape the castle.

Flow Chart

Locomotion Style: Teleportation and smooth locomotion options for player comfort.

Puzzles

Puzzle 1: Escape the Jail Cell

  • Find the hidden key.
  • Use the key to unlock the door and progress to the next puzzle.

Puzzle 2: Find All Key Parts to Open the Door

  • Find a clue and craft a key using resources like iron ingots, axes, and anvils.
  • Arrange stones based on shields to unlock the door.

Puzzle 3: Tavern Room/Lunch Room

  • Find and arrange items like cups, bottles, and plates.
  • Unlock a hidden door by interacting with a book lever.

Puzzle 4: Final Room

  • Follow clues to escape by solving box arrangement puzzles.

Gameplay Scope

  • Game Duration: Approximately 15 minutes.
  • Levels: Four distinct levels.
  • Average Playtime: 10-20 minutes per session.
  • Objectives: Solve puzzles and craft items to escape the castle.
  • Playable Characters: One.
