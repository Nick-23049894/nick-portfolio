About the Project

GitHub Video

The Pokémon Card Manager App is a feature-rich mobile application designed to help users manage their Pokémon cards effortlessly. Whether you're organizing by type, modifying card details, or loading dynamic images, this app provides a seamless experience with intuitive navigation and functionalities.

  • Home Screen: View Pokémon cards categorized by type, such as Water, Fire, Grass, and Rock.
  • Edit Cards: Update Pokémon names and card numbers easily from the edit screen.
  • Delete Cards: Remove unwanted Pokémon cards with a simple delete action and confirmation.
  • Add New Cards: Add new Pokémon cards by entering the name, card number, and type.
  • Dynamic Images: Uses URLs for images, allowing real-time updates and scalable image management.
  • Dynamic Updates: All changes reflect immediately on the home screen for an organized view.

With this app, managing your Pokémon card collection is more enjoyable, efficient, and visually dynamic.
